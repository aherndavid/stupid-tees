How this all happened

Probably a mistake.
But here we are.

After years of wearing t-shirts churned out by corporate designers with zero interest in anything beyond the bland and the forgettable, I'd had enough. So I grabbed a coffee, opened the laptop, and decided to build my own. Brilliant idea. Very sensible.

Every design on this site is 100% mine — no cut and paste, no recycled clip art, no design-by-committee. Each one is made with you in mind, by someone who actually cares what ends up on the front of a tee. Whether that's a good thing is still up for debate.

The range keeps growing, the quality stays high, and every tee ships free within the UK on premium cotton that'll last longer than most of my other decisions. Have a browse. Go on. You've come this far.
