Who we are

Not overthought.
Just well made.

After years of wearing t-shirts churned out by corporate designers with zero interest in anything beyond the bland and the forgettable, I'd had enough. So I grabbed a coffee, opened the laptop, and decided to build my own.

Every design on this site is 100% mine — no cut and paste, no recycled clip art, no design-by-committee. Each one is made with you in mind, by someone who actually cares what ends up on the front of a tee.

The range keeps growing, the quality stays high, and every tee ships free within the UK on premium cotton that'll last. Have a browse, find something you like, and grab yourself a bargain.

Shipping times

All orders are dispatched within 3–5 working days from our UK base. Once shipped, delivery estimates are:

  • UK — 2–3 working days
  • Europe — 5–8 working days
  • Rest of world — 7–14 working days

Returns

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition. To start a return, get in touch via our contact form and we'll sort it out.

Faulty or incorrect items? We'll cover the return postage, no arguments.

Sizing

Our tees run true to size on a standard unisex fit. If you prefer a more fitted look, size down. If you're between sizes, go up. Full size chart available on request.

  • XS — chest 34–36"
  • S — chest 36–38"
  • M — chest 38–40"
  • L — chest 40–42"
  • XL — chest 42–44"
  • XXL — chest 44–46"
